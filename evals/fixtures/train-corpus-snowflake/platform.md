## Enjoy regular, automatic performance improvements and extend your platform

- Experience 2.0x¶  faster performance for core analytics workloads with [Standard Warehouse - Generation 2](https://docs.snowflake.com/en/user-guide/warehouses-gen2), Snowflake’s updated Standard Warehouse with upgraded hardware and additional performance enhancements.

- [Snowflake Adaptive Compute](https://www.snowflake.com/en/product/features/adaptive-compute/)\\\* offers enhanced ease of use with better price for performance through automatic selection of right-sized compute resources shared optimally across an account and intelligent query routing. Use this compute service to create new Adaptive Warehouses.\*
- Snowpark Container Services (SPCS) is a powerful extension that turns Snowflake into a platform for running containers, services and AI/ML apps securely and natively alongside your data.

[Learn about high-performance compute](https://www.snowflake.com/en/webinars/thought-leadership/snowflake-connect-platform-2025-10-28/)

## Run transactional applications, analytics and AI on one unified platform

- Stop managing separate databases. Instead, let Snowflake help unify your data footprint, so you can run transactional workloads alongside all your other workloads on a single platform.

- Choose from two powerful capabilities: [Snowflake Postgres](https://snowflake.com/en/product/features/postgres/) for enterprise-ready Postgres, or [Unistore’s Hybrid Tables](https://www.snowflake.com/en/product/features/unistore/) to manage application state and build lightweight transactional apps directly on Snowflake.

- Eliminate data pipelines to help reduce complexity, lower costs and innovate faster.

Is the Snowflake platform serverless?

Yes, the Snowflake platform is a fully managed service with many serverless capabilities. While you configure virtual warehouses, Snowflake handles the underlying infrastructure, scaling and maintenance.
