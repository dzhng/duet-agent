The opportunity

AI is changing how software is monetized. You need the flexibility to handle new business models, new markets, and the complexity that comes with them.

Consumption-based billing, hybrid models, flexible tiers—pricing models are evolving and you need to keep up. But if your billing infrastructure isn’t flexible enough, every pricing change can slow you down, especially as you expand globally.

Stripe Billing lets you localize pricing, customize billing logic, and collect payments faster. Unify subscriptions and usage-based billing into one automated flow that supports any way you monetize.
